Workflow

How it works

1

Manufacturer submits medicine name, batch ID, and manufacturer name.

2

The data becomes a transaction and is mined into a custom blockchain block.

3

A QR code is saved for the batch so it can be shared or scanned later.

4

Distributor and pharmacy handoffs are stored as more blockchain transactions.

5

Users verify a batch ID. The full history shows Manufacturer -> Distributor -> Pharmacy.
